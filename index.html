Profile

Science credibility, commercial fluency

I trained as a chemical and bioengineer — a BEng from Stellenbosch, an MPhil from Cambridge, and a PhD from Imperial College London in Prof Dame Molly Stevens' group, developing methods to characterise circulating extracellular vesicles as cancer biomarkers. Alongside the science, I built a parallel track in technology transfer: IP protection, patent prosecution, licensing, and spin-out strategy for early-stage life science research. I've since applied machine learning to biomarker discovery as a postdoc at Oxford, and worked start-up-side at a biodiscovery company. I move comfortably between a pipette and a term sheet.
